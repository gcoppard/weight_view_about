Privacy Policy for WeightView

Effective date: November 11, 2025

WeightView (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

  • Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics (linked with Google Analytics) to understand app usage and improve features.
  • Crash reports: We collect anonymized crash data via Firebase Crashlytics to identify and fix bugs.
  • No personal data: We do not collect names, emails, or any personally identifiable information.

How We Use Your Data

We use collected data only to:

  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Understand how users interact with the app
  • Enhance user experience and future updates

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services in our app:

  • Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics)
  • Google Analytics (via Firebase integration)

These services may use cookies or unique device identifiers to analyze usage trends. Data is aggregated and does not personally identify individual users.

Learn more about how Google handles data: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites
Opt out of Google Analytics tracking: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout

Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data. All analytics and crash data collected via Firebase and Google Analytics are handled according to their respective privacy policies and stored securely.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted here with a new effective date. Continued use of the app after an update indicates your acceptance of the revised policy.
